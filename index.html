Description

This is a Pasta Carbonara, a popular dish within italy and is typically made using Guincale

Guincale is cured pork cheek and gives the dish a unique flavour

The method we will be using is not a typical method, as it incorporates making the sauce almost like a hollandaise sauce

Try out this recipe and see how it will change your outlook on Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients

    - 1 lbs Guincale
    - 150 grams Grano Padano Cheese
    - 150 grams Pecorino Romano Cheese
    - Fresh cracked Black Pepper
    - 3 egg yolks
    - High quality Spaghettini noodles
    - Maldon salt to taste

Instructions/Steps

    1. Boil 1.5 L of salted water on the stove
    2. Cut your Guincale into inch cubes
    3. Cook your Guincale off in a pan to medium rare, reserving the fat
    4. Start cooking your pasta to the specified time on the package, ensuring it is al dente
    5. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve the cooking water and keep the noodles hot
    6. Start another pot of water in double boiler fashion
    7. Mix your egg yolks, cheeses, and cracked black pepper over the double boiler
    8. Slowly start adding in your reserved Guincale fat into the cheese and egg mixture
    9. You can also add in some of the reserved cooking water to help thicken the sauce
    10. Once your sauce has achieved desired consistency, you can toss in the cooked noodles and guincale into the double boiler
    11. Finish the dish by stacking the noodles high and adding freshly grated cheeses and freshly cracked black pepper
